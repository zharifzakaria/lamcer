About

Our Story

Laman Cerdik is a family owned kindergarten and nursery schools educating children aged 1 to 10 years old.

In 2005 Hajjah Noorjan Mohamad established the school in Putrajaya and Selangor. Each school provides a warm and welcoming environment. Today, fully accredited by Ministry of Education (Malaysia), Welfare Department (Malaysia) and local authorities, Laman Cerdik is well recognized.

We believe in providing a balanced curriculum that prepares our children academically and nurtures them spiritually. Our curriculum ensures children involvement in a wide range of practical and educational activities that will contribute to the physical growth, social, emotional and the intellectual development of the child.

Our school continues to thrive today because of the vision and dedication of Hajjah Noorjan Mohamad and our team of trained and qualified teachers.

Mission

Our mission is to provide an excellent academic and nursery education in a fun, happy and secure environment to children and firm grounding in moral and ethical values. We pride ourselves on the fact that our children are well prepared for their next school level.

Vision

Laman Cerdik is committed to be the best in educational services and practices.
